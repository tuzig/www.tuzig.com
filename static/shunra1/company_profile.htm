Shunra Software ltd. develops and markets testing and evaluation tools for Internet and Intranet based technologies and products. Using innovative ideas, Shunra's products allow users to accurately evaluate new Wide Area Network (WAN) technologies, prior to full scale deployment.

The immense growth of the Internet, and the Intranet, has brought with it an endless array of new products and technologies. Most of these new comers are immature at introduction, and their ability to deal with  real network conditions is unknown. To solve this problem Shunra Software has developed an emulation module that allows users to test any WAN technology over an emulated network.

Corporate Background

Shunra Software is a privately held company founded in 1997, by a team of experienced management and technical entrepreneurs.  Shunra Software International  headquarters and development center are located in Ra'anana, Israel. Marketing and Customer Support for the U.S. are handled by Shunra's office in Ridgewook, NJ.

The Name - Shunra is the name of a wild-cat in the ancient laguage of aramic used in ancient sumaria.
